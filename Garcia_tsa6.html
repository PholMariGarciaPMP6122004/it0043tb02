Miia

Lamia

Miia (ミーア Mīa) is a Lamia (Snake Girl) and the first girl to be placed with Kurusu as part of the Cultural Exchange Between Species Bill. Despite her seductive and sultry appearance, Miia is very sweet and innocent at heart, often showing affection towards her host and other members of the house.

Rachnera

Arachne

Rachnera (ラクネラ Rakunera) is an Arachne, a spider-like monster girl who was placed with Kurusu after being rescued from a dangerous situation. She is mature and has a teasing personality, often using her web to play tricks on others, but she cares deeply for her new family.

Centorea

Centaur

Centorea Shianus (セントレア・シアヌス Sentorea Shianusu) is a Centaur and a warrior with a noble spirit. She is loyal, brave, and adheres to a code of chivalry, making her a fierce protector of those she cares about.

Mero

Mermaid

Mero (メロ Mero) is a Mermaid who was assigned to live with Kurusu. She has a romantic and slightly melancholic personality, often dreaming of tragic love stories inspired by her favorite fairy tales. Despite her fragile appearance, Mero is kind and optimistic.

Suu

Slime

Suu (スー Sū) is a Slime, a gelatinous creature who can change shape and absorb water to grow larger. Suu is curious, playful, and often acts on her impulses, which sometimes leads to unintended trouble. She is a lovable and essential member of the household.
